Participation Requirements

  • Must have a CodeChef account. If you don't have a CodeChef account, please create one here.
  • Must be registered as a Community Member of CodeChef DDUC Chapter. You can do that here. Once you do that, you will be added to CodeChef Chapter Community social media groups, if not already there.

Rules and Regulations

  • Scores from the 4 monthly contests of CodeChef, i.e, Long Challenge, Lunchtime, Cook-off and Starters, aloong with the contests hosted by DDUC Chapter will be considered.
  • All the scores will be converted to factors of 100, so no change in the scores of Long Challenge, Staters or Lunchtime.
  • For Cook-Off and similar contests with ICPC Ranklists, the final score will be multiplied by 100 and the no. of wrong submissions will be subtracted from it. E.g.: Score of 7(4) will be 700-4 = 796
  • Ties will be resolved on a bi-monthly basis, i.e., every 2 months and will be in form of isolated lockouts between tied players.
  • 1 - 2 month(s) of complete inactivity on CodeChef might result in elimination from the leaderboard, so make sure to participate in at least one contest every month.
